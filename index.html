About Us

Since 2019, Amore Bakery has stood as a beacon of warmth and indulgence near Sri Venkateswara College. Born out of a deep-rooted love for baking, Amore is the brainchild of local culinary enthusiast Rajat Sharma. Every cup and every cake is baked with patience, passion, and precision, capturing both the essence of tradition and a pinch of innovation.

What makes Amore different? It’s the care. Our chocolate is tempered to perfection. Our buns rise slowly overnight. We hand-whisk cream for each cake. Amore isn’t just a bakery — it’s a space of celebration, coffee breaks, friendly conversations, and nostalgia.

From the Founder

"Baking has always been my refuge. With Amore, I wanted to build not just a shop but a story — of flavours, friendships, and fond memories. Each visitor who walks in is a guest in my extended kitchen."

Rajat Sharma, Founder
